How do democracies respond to demographic diversity? Can democracies thrive in heterogeneous societies? How can we build an inclusive democratic authority? As a scholar of Comparative Politics and American Politics, my research examines these questions at the intersection of (de)democratization and demographic diversity through a comparative and historical perspective.

Video Description: In 2023, my close friend Ozkan Yildiz and I created a digital storytelling video that contrasts the personal journeys of my sister and me, which began under similar circumstances but diverged due to the availability—or lack thereof—of accessibility-enhancing technologies. This project received financial and technical support from the Instructional Media Lab at the University of Massachusetts Amherst and was featured at the 2023 Flexible Learning Summit organized by the university.
